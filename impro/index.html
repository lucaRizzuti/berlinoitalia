The object

Not a voucher.
A token.

The Berlino Impro Gutschein is a cast metal token — designed to be held, gifted, and used. It comes mounted on a card with a ribbon strap, ready to give.

It is not disposable. It has weight. It means something.

Accepted by

The Gutschein is valid at Berlino Italia Improv events and accepted by our partner improvisation schools in Berlin.

To redeem a voucher (Gutschein / buono) with us or with one of our partners, you must contact the respective partner in advance. This applies both to booking a class and to attending a theatre performance.
