Gutschein

A metal token. A gift that opens two doors — a seat at the theatre for two, or a spot in a drop-in improvisation class.

How it works
01 🎭

Go to a show
for two

Use one Gutschein to attend any of our monthly shows at Teatro Acud — and bring someone with you. One token, two seats. No booking, no fuss.

02 ⚡

Join a
drop-in class

Use your token to walk into one of our drop-in improvisation sessions — no previous experience needed, no commitment. Just show up and play.

The object

Not a voucher.
A token.

The Berlino Impro Gutschein is a cast metal token — designed to be held, gifted, and used. It comes mounted on a card with a ribbon strap, ready to give.

It is not disposable. It has weight. It means something.
